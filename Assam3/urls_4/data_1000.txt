Sonapur: In a series of significant community engagement initiatives and as a part of the series of village OPDs under the Medical Civic Action Program in North East region,1st Battalion Sashastra Seema Bal led by Sunil Kaushik, Commandant,
1st BN SSB Sonapur conducted medical village OPDs at Khudra Katala Baharkuch and Namati village under Nalbari district.
Dr Nirjanta Devi, AC(MO) took the initiative in conducting the medical camp assisted by SSB personnel of C Company Nalbari.Villagers,media persons and other prominent persons were present during the medical camp.Around 100 villagers were benefited by the camp.
