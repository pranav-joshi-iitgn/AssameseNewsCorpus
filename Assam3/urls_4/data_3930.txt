Assam: Kaziranga National Park launches onlice ticketing process
Guwahati: The online ticket booking process opened for tourists in Kaziranga National Park from today, official said on Saturday.

Tourists can enter the national park by booking tickets themselves for kaziranga national park safari.
Taking to Twitter, the official account of Kaziranga National Park wrote, “Kaziranga National Park has now gone online with its entry ticket booking. Now book your tickets from anywhere and enjoy seamless and cashless safari experience.”

At Mihimukh in Kaziranga, DFO Ramesh Kumar Gogoi of Kaziranga National Park officially launched the online ticket ordering system.Divisional Forest Officer (DFO) Ramesh Kumar Gogoi officially inaugurated the online ticket booking process at Mihimukh in Kaziranga.

“Local and foreign tourists will be able to do safari by booking tickets in advance through www.kazirangasafari.in website”, official said
