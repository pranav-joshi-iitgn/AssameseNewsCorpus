In a series of significant community engagement initiatives and welfare programmes under its Civic Action Program in NE,1st Battalion Sashastra Seema under the command of Sh. Anuj Kumar, Officiating Commandant conducted a vocational training on tailoring at Sariahtali village under its C Coy Nalbari.
The opening ceremony was attended by Secretary,Sariahtali Village Committee and other prominent persons of the village and the women trainees. Sewing machines will be distributed after the completion of the training.
Such events create and develop a good sense of security among the local people and SSB.The SSB is always with the common people and doing its best for the welfare of the local people in the area.
