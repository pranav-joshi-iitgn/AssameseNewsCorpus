1st BN SSB Sonapur conducted Free Medical Civic Action under Civic Action Program in NE
Sonapur, 01 March: To uphold the motto of Sashastra Seema Bal: Service, Security and Brotherhood, 1st Bn Sashastra Seema Bal Sonapur organized Free Medical camp under Civic Action programme in North East Region under the guidance of Shri Sunil Kaushik, Commandant,1st BN SSB Sonapur assisted by Dr.
Th. Nirjanta Devi, Asstt. Commandant (Medical Officer) at Tengaguri & Morikolong village, District Morigaon under AOR of B- Coy Morigaon in which Villagers, media persons and other prominent persons were present during the medical camp.
This medical camp provided basic health care services and free medicines were also distributed to the local villagers. Around 63 villagers benefited from this camp.
