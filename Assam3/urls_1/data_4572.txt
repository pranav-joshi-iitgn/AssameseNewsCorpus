RJ Mandy from Guwahati,Assam wins Unicef Radio For Child Award in Mumbai,India.
RJ Mandy from Radio Gupshup 94.3 FM,Assam,North East Wins Unicef Radio For Child Award today for the Best Spot for Climate Change in Mumbai conferred by Unicef Celebrity Ambassador Ayushmann Khurana.

22 radio professionals from all over India from All India Radio, Community Radio and Private FM, took away the Radio4ChildAwards 2024 trophy today given away by renowned Bollywood actor and UNICEF India National Ambassador #AyushmannKhurrana, in Mumbai today.
The Awards were given across three themes – #RoutineImmunization, #ClimateChange and #ChildProtection. RJ Mandy has a Radio Experience of 17 years and is currently The Cluster Programming Head and Hosts The RJ Mandy Show every Morning from 7am to 12pm on Radio Gupshup 94.3 FM Proud to be Local!
