AFC C Diploma Course organized by the Guwahati City FC (GCFC) began today at the Sonapur Stadium. The course which is being organised in association with Sonapur Sports Association and DBI, Sonapur is being attended by 24 coaches across north east. All India Football Federation instructor C. Rabisankar Singh is leading the course which shall conclude on 11 April.
GCFC Directors Darick Ranjan Deka, Kaustab Chakraborty and club physio Dr Jeuti Sarma attended the opening ceremony today.
