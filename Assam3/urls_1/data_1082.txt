1st Bn SSB Sonapur organised closing ceremony of Vocational training on Tailoring conducted for 30 days benefitted 35 women under Civic Action Programme in NE region which was scheduled wef. 28/02/2024 to 29/03/2024 in coordination with Rural Development and Self Employment Training Institute (RSETI) Paikarkuchi, Nalbari. The training period ended successfully and closing ceremony was organised to facilitate the participants and note the success of this important achievement.

Shri Bidut Bikas Gogoi, RSETI Director and Inspector (GD) Amarendra Gogoi distributed certificates to the women trainees and encouraged them to remain focused on their objectives for setting up their own enterprise in the future.
