An Agniveer pre-recruitment training camp for Adivasi and Tea Garden Community youths was conducted at Hatigarh Army Camp in Udalguri today . The initiative was organised in Collaboration with the Aadhunik India Foundation. The training program aimed at preparing young army aspirants for physical, medical, and written tests.
Nearly 244 youths, including girls from different Tea Garden under the districts of Udalguri, participated in the preliminary screening tests, creating a festive and enthusiastic atmosphere among the youths .
The Conducting officer from ARMY stated that the dedicated and experienced staff from the Army will be preparing raw youth to meet standard requirements in becoming an Agniveer. All the information about Army recruitment was provided by power point presentation to the aspirants.
On this occasion, National President of Aadhunik India Foundation, DK Chauhan also encouraged all the Adivasi and Tea Garden Community youths to join the Indian Army.
