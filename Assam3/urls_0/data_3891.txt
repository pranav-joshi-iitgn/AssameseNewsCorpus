The Assam Football Association proudly announces the successful conclusion of the Assam Futsal Championship 2023-24. Commencing on January 20th, the tournament featured eight competitive teams.
In a thrilling semifinal clash, Rihan FC secured their place in the final by triumphing over Tribal FC with a scoreline of 7-4. Guwahati City FC demonstrated their prowess by overcoming Assam Rhino FC with a commanding 7-2 victory.
The championship culminated in an intense final between Rihan FC and Guwahati City FC. In a nail-biting encounter, Guwahati City FC emerged victorious, clinching the title with a narrow 4-3 scoreline.
The Assam Football Association extends its congratulations to Guwahati City FC for their exceptional performance and expresses gratitude to all participating teams for contributing to the success of the inaugural Assam Futsal Championship.
