The opening ceremony of vocational training on tailoring in coordination with RSETI Nalbari for 35 women under the Civic Action Programme in NE was held today at the RSETI Institute,Paikarkuchi Nalbari.This one month training programme on tailoring for the unemployed women who are under privileged and BPL will continue till 29 March 2024.The opening function commenced with the lightning of the lamp by Sh. Sunil Kaushik,Commandant 1st BN SSB Sonapur followed by a short prayer. Sh. Sarvesh Vijayant,LDM and Sh.
Bidut Bikas Gogoi, Director RSETI Nalbari, Sh. Kishore Kumar, President Human Rights Commission Nalbari Circle and other dignitaries and media persons also attended the program. Sh. Sunil Kaushik,Commandant 1BN SSB in his welcome speech encouraged the women trainees to enhance their livelihoods by making most of such training programmes and remained focused on their objectives for setting up their own enterprises in the near future.
