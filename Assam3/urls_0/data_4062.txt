Assam’s tennis team, represented by Hakim Ali, Purna Jyoti Phukan, Dhruba Jyoti Hazarika and Diganta Borpatra Gohain (all from Duliajan) shines in the 6th National Masters Games -2024 held at Goa from February 06-12, 2024. In the Men’s Singles (above 55 years) category Hakim Ali clinched double crowns by winning gold medal in the singles and also won gold in the doubles categories respectively partnering Purna Jyoti Phukan. In the Men’s Singles (above 55 years) category Purna Jyoti Phukan won a bronze medal.
In the Men’s Singles (above 50 years) category Dhruba Jyoti Hazarika won bronze medal and while partnering Diganta Borpatra Gohain in the doubles in the same category also won the bronze medal.
