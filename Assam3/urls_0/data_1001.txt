In continuation to various veterinary village OPDs as a part of Veterinary Civic Action Programme in NE region,1st BN Sashastra Seema Bal, Sonapur conducted veterinary village OPDs here at three villages Sariahtali, Barsarkuchi and Kashempur under Nalbari District.
The veterinary camp was organised under the guidance of Sunil Kaushik,Commandant,1st BN SSB. Dr. KK Singh,Vety Comndt SSB took the initiative in conducting the VCA programme assisted by company personnel of C Coy,Nalbari.
Nearly 100 animals were treated during the camp.Villagers,SSB personnel and media persons were present. SSB is always with the common people and doing its best for the welfare of the local people with the motto “Service Security and Brotherhood”.
